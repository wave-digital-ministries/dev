Privacy

We do not track you or your devices. Our websites are tracker and cookie free!
The only information about you that we collect is the information that you submit to us in our Planning Center or Hubspot forms. Our Hubspot forms (Trouble tickets, feedback, and bug reports) have the option not to input private information. The information collected is kept secure and will never be sold, distributed, or shared.
